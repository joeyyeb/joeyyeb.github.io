01 — Story

I grew up in Ghana.
I think globally.

I'm Joseph. A builder at heart, based in Accra, Ghana — working with teams and companies across the world.

My path hasn't been a straight line. I started as an IT auditor at Deloitte, learning how systems break and how to make them stronger. Then I moved into product — spending two years as a remote Product Manager at a FinTech startup, working with engineers and designers to ship things people actually use.

Right now I'm leading operations at a family agrochemical business. It's old school in the best and most challenging way — and I've been quietly modernising it from the inside. Building dashboards where there were spreadsheets. Turning gut feel into data. Making decisions feel less like guesswork.

One day I want to build my own startup. Until then, I'm sharpening every skill I can — product, data, operations, leadership — and looking for the right remote team to grow with.

Ghana is home. The world is the market.
